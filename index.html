About

This September, 30 organisational hackers and innovators will gather for 4 days in the beautiful Belgian countryside. Enspiral Org Academy is a unique training in future ways of working: ways that are more human, self-managing, non-hierarchical, agile and deliberately developmental.

Many of us know that “business as usual” in our organisations and workplaces is not sustainable anymore. We want to introduce new ways of working and learn to lead in a new paradigm way - but it’s difficult to find out how to do that.

Many have read the books - Reinventing Organisations by Fredric Laloux or Brave New Work by Aaron Dignan - but few have lived the experience. How do I build a team or company that is self-managing and non-hierarchical but doesn’t descend into chaos? How do I lead in a way that is more horizontal and less top-down?

Join us at this immersive training and retreat to learn new things, practice different patterns, meet fellow The Enspiral Org Academy is for people who are already working in non-hierarchical environments, or just starting their journey into self-management. You might be a founder, coach, manager, organisational designer, or community builder.

Who is this for?

Are you an organisational innovator? Are you curious about new ways of working? Do you feel like work could be the place we grow ourselves and our capacity to lead, regarle

Perhaps you have already started working horizontally or self-managing in some ways. This training will strengthen, accelerate and deepen what you have begun. You will learn practices to help you where you are stuck.

Perhaps you are an employee, founder or manager and you want to find out ways you can encourage your team to be more engaged, have more agency, and freedom.

What we will cover

We’ve prepared a selection of facilitated training sessions to share what we’ve learned about some of the most difficult challenges of collaborative organisation:

Decision-making

protocols and practices for making quick, inclusive decisions, face-to-face and online

Culture

growing trust & psychological safety; effective communication & relational skills

Self

shifting mindsets towards a new paradigm; designing orgs for wellbeing and development

Power

identifying different group power dynamics to move beyond hidden hierarchies

Roles

distributing work; accountability practices; balancing alignment & autonomy

Money

transparency; co-budgeting; and the psychology of financial collaboration

What will you get out of it?

  • Lived, practical experience of non-hierarchical ways of working alongside experienced people who can help you on your specific challenges.

  • A toolkit of methodologies and practices to take back into your workplace.

  • Knowledge from research and case studies of other organisations experimenting with radical forms of management and work culture.

  • The key ingredients and patterns to start your own decentralised Enspiral-like network or community.

  • An intimate space where you will be supported to pause and reflect, but also encouraged to stretch yourself and learn.

  • A unique opportunity to hear from the many organisational experiments we’ve implemented at Enspiral; we’ll share the highs as well as the lows.

  • Deep new connections with brilliant inspiring people aligned in work and purpose!
